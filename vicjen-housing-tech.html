The Problem
Why this exists

Land and housing in Nigeria carry risks that shouldn't exist

Disputed titles, unverified sellers, guesswork valuations, and a housing market that prices people out of safety. We built VICJEN Housing Tech to remove these, one transaction at a time.

Our Story
Anambra State, 2026

"We built the company we needed when we had nothing."

Victor & Jennifer

Built from displacement, by two people who found each other in it

Victor and Jennifer were both displaced as children. Both lost their fathers to the consequences of housing insecurity, the kind that follows a family long after the event itself. Years later, fate brought them together, and they discovered how closely their stories matched.

They married, and chose to build one company that could answer both of their lives at once. A business with the rigor to acquire, value, and develop property properly, and the conviction to make sure fewer families face what theirs did. Long before there was a company, there was the habit of paying rent for families who needed it, quietly, because they understood what it cost not to.

VICJEN Housing Tech is that answer: technology and capital pointed at the same problem that shaped its founders.
